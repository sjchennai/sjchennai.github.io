Message from our directors!

  • Study after study shows quality preschool is an educational springboard that prepares children to learn and succeed in education, careers, and life. Neuroscientists have found that early experiences, particularly from birth to age five, shape a child's brain and development and can have long-lasting effects, ultimately preparing children for a brighter future.

    "Shanti Juniors" is a well known chain of play school addressing the educational needs of early childhood years by following developmentally appropriate practices. We celebrate childhood through the unique blend of international approaches amalgamated with Indian culture. We provide age appropriate early childhood care and education programs integrated with technology in a joyful environment to lay a strong foundation for our happy and righteous children to be responsible citizens of the world.

    At our Alwarthirunagar center, we are committed to bringing the highest quality of early childhood education and care for your children and your family. We started this preschool with a mission to provide un-compromised education and care to every single child in the center. Our utmost priority is to provide a safe, healthy, comfortable and friendly environment for every child. We believe that only when a child feels comfortable and happy, he/she starts learning at an unimaginable pace. We carefully handpick qualified, caring and dedicated staff to ensure that every single child is given individual attention.

    We assure you that you will never regret for your decision to enrol your child here at our center. We are happy to clarify any of your questions, hence free to contact us.

    G.Murugesan and V.Shreedharan
    Retired Engineers of Hindustan Teleprinters Ltd (Govt of India).

Our Center

  • Our Alwarthirunagar center, is located in a quiet and less polluted residential neighbourhood. Our beautiful, comfortable environment is safe, nurturing, and specifically designed to optimize the development of young hearts and minds. We focus on developing the child's academic, physical, social and language skills that will enable them to be successful in the future.

    We respect the unique set of talents and challenges for each child. Our teachers and caretakers are trained to help each child play, learn and explore new skills and activities. They recognize each success, challenge, and effort, and reflect these back to the children. In such a personally supportive environment, children feel secure and optimistic. They seek out new challenges and learn how to solve problems.

    Our aim is to provide un-compromised education and care to every single child in the center. Our utmost priority is to provide a safe, healthy, comfortable and friendly environment for every child. We believe that only when a child feels comfortable and happy, he/she starts learning at an unimaginable pace.
